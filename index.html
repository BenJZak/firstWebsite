About Me

Most of my free time is spent learning about great thinkers/writers such as: Albert Camus, Aristotle, and Lucius Annaeus Seneca. While I may be introverted by nature, I have big plans for the future and am constantly seeking out new opportunities to grow and learn. My sole aim is to constantly improve and become the best version of myself that I can be. I believe that by consistently working on myself and striving for personal growth, I can reach my full potential and lead a fulfilling and meaningful life. I'm a big fan of nature, hiking, and skiing, although I'm definitely not an expert in any of those areas. I enjoy getting out there and getting some exercise, and I find that these activities are a great way to relax and pass the time. Whether I'm taking it easy on a scenic trail or tackling a more challenging route, I always find a way to have a good time and stay active.
